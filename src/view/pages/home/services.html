What does UniConnect has to offer?

  • UniConnect is offering university profiles and courses available produced from the inputted information from users.
  • Information available in this app is for the Australian and New Zealand universities only but its accessible by any foreign student aspirants anywhere and can be subscribed by any current international students located in these two counties.

Active 24/7 Chatbot Support

A chatbot is a software system, which can interact or “chat” with a human user in natural in universal English language. Chatbots can help inform users or help them in fulfilling tasks.

UniConnect has built-in chatbot feature capable of simulating a conversation with a person. It has an artificial intelligence software designed to perform a series of tasks on its own and without the help of a human being. This feature of the UniConnect will assist the need of foreign students in addressing pre-arrival, study, and cultural challenges once reached the university in Australia and New Zealand.






Comprehensive Australian and New Zealand List Universities

Compare universities around Australia and New Zealand with our interactive university search tool. Information available at this time is for the two countries mentioned but its accessible by any foreign student aspirants anywhere and subscribe by any current international students located in these two counties.

UniConnect offers information comprising list of visa and school requirements of the chosen university to study. This app will help tracking the progress of the applicants in complying all the requirements.







Connection Building Capabilities

It provides location and cultural overview of the university that have been chosen during pre-arrival processing.
