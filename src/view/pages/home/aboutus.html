About UniConnect

International students make a major contribution to any first-class country (North America, Western Europe, Japan and Australia) around the world.All members of the creator of UniConnect are international students that have experienced directly difficulty from initial stage of the process from getting initial school and visa requirements, processing time spent and misleading information.

UniConnect will primarily supply the information about Australian and New Zealand universities that will influence them in choosing the course to study and inject information about the location prior flying overseas to study. UniConnect is being developed to offer sustainable support to international students by establishing their own network and addressing educational and cultural issues that arise when uni life commences. The application has embedded chatbot inspired by AI to have a real time interaction to users.
